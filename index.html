Grapevine is a place for

As citizens we always hear others complain about politics and say things like “the system is broken” or “they don’t represent us.” We complain but we don’t really do anything to change the situation. We just elect new people into the same system.

Grapevine is here to change the situation and help the system. Grapevine is a site that promotes nonpartisan civic engagement in Ohio. Grapevine doesn’t want to change how legislation is officially voted on. Instead, Grapevine wants to create a new digital tool for citizens to use to participate in democracy. A digital tool that modernizes the way we interact with representatives because the current methods are inefficient.

Think about the current ways to contact a representative: telephone, email, letter, and fax. They can be good ways to contact a representative, but they all require serious manual labor if you want to keep record of people’s opinions. This is a difficult task when representatives in Ohio represent an average of 116,000 people. It makes sense to build a digital tool like Grapevine where technology can assist with processing the information.

What We Offer

Education

Anyone who visits Grapevine can currently browse through proposed legislation in Ohio and search for legislation via keywords. This is the starting point for having more informed citizens. Even if most Grapevine users don’t want to research legislation themselves, they can still learn about legislation from other users that do research the legislation. They can do this through the next component of the site, the discussion board.

Discussion

Coming soon. Registered voters in Ohio will be able to sign up for free and access a statewide discussion board to discuss anything related to Ohio. Here users will be able to learn from each other and promote positive and educational conversation. There will be rules and a three strike system that results in bans from the discussion board.

Data

Coming soon. Registered voters in Ohio will be able to sign up for free and access unofficial voting on proposed legislation. Using the results from this unofficial voting we will be able to generate important and accurate data that helps citizens and their representatives.

Decisions

Grapevine’s ultimate goal is to be a tool that supplements representative democracy. We want to start by helping the people of Ohio stay engaged with their state politics. We want citizens and representatives to be able to make more informed decisions when voting.
